Top notes are otherwise called the head notes.

The scents that are perceived immediately on application of a perfume. Top notes consist of small, light molecules that evaporate quickly. They form a person's initial impression of a perfume and thus are very important in the selling of a perfume. Citrus and ginger scents are common top notes.
