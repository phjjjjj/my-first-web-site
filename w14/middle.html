Middle note

Middle notes are also called the "heart notes", the middle notes are the scent of a perfume that emerges just before the top notes dissipate.

The middle note compounds form the "heart" or main body of a perfume and act to mask the often unpleasant initial impression of base notes, which become more pleasant with time. Examples of middle notes include seawater, sandalwood, rose and jasmine.
